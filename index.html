Dunder Mifflin Paper Company, Inc. is a fictional paper sales company featured in the American television series The Office.The company was depicted as based in New York City, with branches in smaller Northeastern cities. Episodes are set in the Scranton branch, but other branches have been mentioned and seen.

Michael Gary Scott (born March 15, 1962, in Scranton, Pennsylvania), is a fictional character played by Steve Carell, who is a former branch manager of the Scranton branch of Dunder Mifflin Paper Company, The Michael Scott Paper Company, and later Dunder Mifflin Sabre.
